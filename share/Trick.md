## Disable adobe right-click context menu
```Powershell
cd 'C:\Program Files (x86)\Adobe\Acrobat DC\Acrobat Elements'
regsvr32.exe /u ContextMenuShim64.dll
regsvr32.exe /u ContextMenu64.dll
regsvr32.exe /u ContextMenu.dll
regsvr32.exe /u ContextMenu.dll
```
